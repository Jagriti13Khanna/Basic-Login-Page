Design Principle of Alignment

Alignment gives our design unity and organization and we can achieve it by creating strong edges and connecting elements within the design to something else on the page. Alignment enhances readability and allows our audience to easily find information. The alignment principle can be compared to the example of a messy closet vs a tidy, well organized closet.
